If you are tested positive for COVID 19

• Stay at home

• Take some rest and stay hydrated

• Stay in touch with your doctor

• Have medicines recommended by your doctor

• Try to avoid contact with people and pets in your home

• Follow COVID 19 Guidelines


If you are tested negative for COVID-19

• You might not be infected of the virus at the time you were tested. However, you were probably early in your phase of infection; therefore, you might be tested positive later

• It is important to keep in mind that if you get a negative test result, there are chances you'll get sick.

• If you were in contact with someone that has COVID-19, you should quarantine even though you have a negative test result.

• Follow COVID 19 Guidelines
