Click here for more information about at-home testing.

If you test positive for COVID-19:

• Stay at home.

• Take rest and stay hydrated.

• Keep in contact with your doctor.

• Take medicine recommended by your doctor.

• Avoid contact with people and pets in your home.

• Follow these COVID-19 Guidelines.


If you test negative for COVID-19

• You may have not been infected at the time of testing. However, you may have been in an early phase of infection; therefore, you might test positive later.

• Keep in mind that if you test negative, there are still chances that you will get infected.

• If you were in contact with someone that has COVID-19, you should quarantine despite receiving a negative test result.

• Follow these COVID-19 Guidelines.
