About Us



For over 15 years, Metelehealth has been making a difference in the lives of our patients, caregivers, and staff. Our international suite of services includes personal healthcare, realtime assistance, and around the clock counseling. Our commitment to customer service and patient care needs makes us a trusted partner wherever healthcare is needed.

Dr. Prejeet Patel I graduated from Harvard Medical School in 1975 with a Doctorate in Medicine. He was a small man with a big dream and founded METelehealth in 2006. Ever since, he has made it Metelehealth's mission to aid, accommodate, and assist the public with their medical needs.
