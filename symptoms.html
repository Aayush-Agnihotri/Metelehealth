Symptoms

Symptoms:

  • Fever or chills
  • Cough
  • Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing
  • Fatigue
  • Muscle or body aches
  • Headaches
  • New loss of taste or smell
  • Sore throat
  • Congestion or runny nose
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Diarrhea

    • Seek care if you are experiencing:

  • Trouble breathing
  • Persistent pain or pressure in your chest
  • New confusion
  • The inability to wake or stay awake
  • Pale, gray, or blue-colored skin, lips, or nail beds

    • 1. Quarantine from people and wear a mask around cohabitants.

    2. Attempt to schedule a COVID-19 test if need be.

    3. Contact your local health provider for further advice and individual quarantine guidelines.
